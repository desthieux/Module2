The second France-China Leaders High-Level Meeting for Business Leaders will take place on

13th & 14th November 2014 in Bordeaux

An initiative of Club XXIe Siècle

The France-China Leaders (FCL) Summit is an initiative of Club XXIe Siècle. It aims to provide an exclusive networking platform for business leaders-senior executives and successful entrepreneurs from listed companies in France and China, adress key issues and enhance business cooperation between France and China
